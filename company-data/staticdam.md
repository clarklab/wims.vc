## The Elevator Pitch

<p class="elevator-pitch">A modern Digital Asset Management system for saving, organizing, and sharing images and video, built for small and quick marketing teams.</p>

## The Vision

Any marketing team with more than 1 person already has a Dropbox, and a Google Drive, and STILL no one knows which of the latest stock photos to use. Get everyone on the same page, powered by Git.

## The Opportunity

Quick! Go Google other DAM solutions, I'll wait. They are SO expensive! I'm sure they are great, but for a small to medium team, a one-time setup fee might be EXTREMELY attractive.
