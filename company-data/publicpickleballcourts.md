## The Elevator Pitch

<p class="elevator-pitch">The most comprehensive directory of public pickleball courts nationwide. Thousands of locations with reviews, amenities, and availability.</p>

## The Vision

Every city in America has a pair of pickleball courts. Major cities have dozens. Help super-DUPR pickleball players find their next game or tournament.

## The Opportunity

A true local boots-on-the-ground approach, onboarding cities with the help of a local pickleball diehard. Local maintainers combining for national impact. Sponsored listing, featured tournaments, grand openings, we got it covered.

Plus we have one of the top **pickleball search terms** as our plaintext domain: welcome to Public Pickleball Courts dot com.
