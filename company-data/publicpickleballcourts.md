## The Opportunity

Pickleball is the fastest-growing sport in America (14.8M players in 2023, up 158% in 3 years). Public Pickleball Courts is the most comprehensive directory of courts nationwide, with location data, reviews, and real-time availability.

## What's Built

- **Interactive map** - Mapbox integration with 10,000+ court locations
- **User reviews** - Community-driven ratings and updates
- **Search & filter** - Find courts by location, amenities, surface type
- **Mobile-optimized** - Progressive web app for on-the-go discovery
- **SEO-optimized** - Ranking for "pickleball courts near me" and local searches
